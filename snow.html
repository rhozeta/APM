Snow Removal

When winter arrives, we take care of your snow removal needs. We offer full winter commercial snow plowing services for our clients. Ajax Property Maintenance can provide you with 24-hour snow plowing services throughout the entire winter season.

Our specialists will survey the property to establish the most efficient way to keep your lot snow-free. Ajax Property Maintenance provides salt and other melting agents as necessary. We only use commercial grade snow plows, and unauthorized use of equipment is strictly prohibited.

Services Include:
  • Plowing all parking lots
  • Shovelling of all entranceways, walkways, and other pedestrian areas
  • 24 x 7 Spot Checks
  • Salting when required
  • Salt bins on location (by request only)

In addition to making your property more tidy, our winter snow removal services ensures that your grounds are safe from slips, falls, and other accidents.

For even further peace of mind, Ajax Property Maintenance carries $5 million in public liability insurance.
